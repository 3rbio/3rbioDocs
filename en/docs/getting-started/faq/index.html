Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ section is designed to provide answers to common questions that players have about the game.

How do I start playing the game?

To start playing 3rb.io, simply visit our website at 3rb.io using your web browser. No downloads or installations are required — just choose a username and start playing instantly! .

Is the game free?

Yes! 3rb.io is completely free to play. You can enjoy the game anytime without paying any fees or needing to do anything extra.

How can I grow in the game?

To grow bigger and stronger, you need to absorb players who are smaller than you. The more cells you consume, the more your mass and size increase — giving you the chance to dominate other players.

Can I play with my friends?

Yes! You can invite your friends to join by sharing the game link 3rb.io or by creating an invite code to play together. Compete in real-time and enjoy the interactive gameplay while climbing the leaderboard together!

Are there leaderboards or competitions in the game?

Absolutely! The game features a leaderboard that displays the top players during each session. The stronger you get, the closer you'll be to reaching the top! Challenge others and aim to be the best.

Why isn’t login working right now?

We're still working on setting up and refining the login system and the in-game store. For now, you can play without needing to log in. Once everything is ready, the store will be opened, and everyone will be able to log in and explore its features.

Are there paid features in the game?

The game is completely free to play. However, there are some optional cosmetic items you can purchase to enhance your appearance in-game. These items don’t affect gameplay or your progress — they’re purely for looks. Login and store features will be launched later this year.
