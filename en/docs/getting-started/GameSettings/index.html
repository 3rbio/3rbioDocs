Game Settings

The 3rb.io game settings allow you to customize your gameplay experience to match your style. From adjusting controls etc.., everything you need is right here.

Gameplay​

info

Skip Match Results!

At the end of each game — whether you die or the timer runs out — you'll see a Match Results screen. It displays detailed stats from your session, including your total play time, food eaten, highest mass reached, survival time, cells eaten, and your top position on the leaderboard.

info

Render Options!

Customize how the game looks and feels with various visual settings. You can toggle options like:

  • Show Names
  • Show Skins
  • Show Wearables
  • Animation Speed
  • Auto Zoom
  • Mouse Arrow
  • Show Mass
  • Hide Food
  • Hide Border

These settings help you tailor the visual experience to your preferences and performance needs.

info

UI Options!

Control the user interface elements to reduce distractions or improve performance. Available options include:

  • Hide XP Bar – Removes the experience bar from the screen.

  • Hide Chat – Hides the in-game chat for a cleaner interface.

Themes​

info

Theme!

Choose your preferred background style to customize the look of the game. Available options include:

  • Hexagon – A stylish hex-pattern background.
  • Lines – A lined grid-style background.
  • NoN-Style – The standard 3rb.io background.

Color!

Select the overall color scheme for the game interface:

  • Dark
  • White
  • Black

Controls​

danger

Reset Controls – Restore all control settings to default.

info

Customize and reset your in-game controls for the best gameplay experience. Available actions include:

  • Feed – Assign a key to eject mass.
  • Split – Perform a single split.
  • Double Split – Split twice quickly.
  • Triple Split – Split three times in succession.
  • 16x Split – Execute a full 16-cell split.
  • Freeze (Line Split) – Temporarily freeze for precise line splits.
  • Vertical (Line Split) – Perform a vertical line split.
  • Hide UI (For Clips) – Hide all interface elements for clean gameplay recording or screenshots.

Region​

info

Currently, the game runs on a single server located in Europe. More regions may be added in the future to improve global performance.
